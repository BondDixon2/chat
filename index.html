What is Kyvex Chat?

Kyvex Chat is a revolutionary chat application that prioritizes user privacy and freedom. Unlike many chat apps, Kyvex Chat is completely open source and free forever. With Kyvex Chat, you can easily connect with friends and enjoy seamless communication without worrying about your data being sold to advertisers or collected by third-party entities. Our platform is dedicated to providing a secure and private space for users to chat, share files, and engage with each other in a fun and convenient way. With Kyvex Chat, you can experience the benefits of an innovative and user-friendly chat app that is free from hidden fees, data breaches, and invasive surveillance.

Features

Chat with friends

With Kyvex Chat, you can easily chat with your friends and stay connected, no matter where you are in the world.

Stay private

Kyvex Chat is designed with your privacy in mind. We never collect or sell your data, so you can chat with confidence.

Open source

Our code is fully open source and available on GitHub, so you can see exactly how we're keeping your data secure.

Customize your experience

Kyvex Chat is highly customizable, allowing you to personalize your chat experience to suit your unique needs and preferences.

No hidden fees

Kyvex Chat is 100% free, with no hidden fees or charges. We believe that everyone should have access to a great chat app, without having to pay for it.

Cross-platform

Our app works seamlessly across multiple platforms, including desktop and mobile devices, so you can stay connected no matter where you are.
